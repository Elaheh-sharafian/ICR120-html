Acupuncture:

Acupuncture is an ancient Chinese medical system that uses the careful insertion of fine, sterile needles into specific points of the body that control the flow of “Qi” energy and brings about better health.

Breathwork:

Breathwork is a powerful tool for almost anyone. It helps free up stagnant energy, release emotional pain and balance emotions, energize us and revitalize our bodies. Breath can be a gauge of life force and it is the only autonomous bodily function we can control so learning to breathe well is key to good health.
